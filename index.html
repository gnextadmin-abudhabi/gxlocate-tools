The opportunity

A broad catalogue needs a focused launch.

Oasis Cart spans fashion, electronics, home, beauty and daily essentials. The first quarter will identify the hero products, messages and audiences that can acquire profitable customers—without wasting budget across every category at once.

01

Trust before scale

Use product proof, fulfilment clarity, authentic reviews, payment safety, returns and responsive support to overcome new-brand hesitation.

02

Orders, not vanity installs

Optimize around first purchase, order value, contribution margin and repeat purchase—not app-install volume alone.

03

Hero-category discipline

Launch with 10–20 high-potential products selected by demand, stock, margin, price competitiveness and delivery reliability.

Google commerce foundation

Merchant Center and tracking, set up properly.

Catalogue growth depends on accurate products, destinations, conversion values and stock—not simply switching campaigns on.

Google Merchant Center

  • Business profile and access setup
  • UAE shipping, returns and support details
  • Website verification and claim
  • Google Ads and GA4 linking
  • Scheduled feed/API/platform connection
  • Titles, descriptions, images and identifiers
  • Price and stock synchronization
  • Diagnostics and eligible issue resolution
  • Custom labels for margin, category and stock

Purchase-level measurement

  • Firebase/GA4 event architecture
  • First open, sign-up and product-view events
  • Wishlist, add-to-cart and checkout events
  • Purchase order ID, value, currency and category
  • Refund/cancellation reconciliation where possible
  • Google on-device iOS measurement guidance
  • Apple privacy attribution compatibility
  • Meta/TikTok app event and attribution QA
  • Dashboard by platform, emirate, category and creative
Merchant eligibility conditionAn App Store listing alone is not a product landing page. Shopping/free listings generally require a verified transactional website with matching products, prices, availability, policies and checkout. Oasis Cart must also confirm its direct-seller or merchant-of-record model because unrelated third-party marketplace structures can be restricted. Setup is included; platform approval cannot be guaranteed.
